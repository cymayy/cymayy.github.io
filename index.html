My Background

With a degree in computer science and a passion for technology and design, I founded my own software development company. Through hard work and dedication, I have created applications that solve real-world problems and help people.

TextLab App

TextLab is my flagship product. It is an intuitive app that lets users enhance images by adding customizable text overlays. Whether for social media, marketing, or personal use, TextLab provides the tools needed to create captivating visuals.

Key Features:

  • Variety of font styles and sizes
  • Adjustable text color, opacity, and alignment
  • Resize, rotate, and position text
  • Layer multiple text boxes on a single image
  • Save edited images directly to your device

I am proud to have developed this app from scratch, handling everything from the initial concept to the final product. TextLab represents my commitment to creating innovative software that improves the lives of users.
